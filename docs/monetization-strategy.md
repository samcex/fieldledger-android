# FieldLedger Monetization Strategy

## Positioning

FieldLedger sells recurring admin relief for solo tradespeople. The value proposition is not generic bookkeeping. It is "log the job on-site, know what to invoice, and see what still needs follow-up."

## Free Plan

- Up to 15 saved jobs
- Core dashboard metrics
- Local-only storage on device

## Pro Plan

- `field_ledger_pro_monthly`
- `field_ledger_pro_yearly`
- Unlimited jobs and customers
- Four-week revenue trends
- Outstanding invoice visibility
- Future invoice export and reminder tools

## Pricing

- Monthly: `$6.99`
- Yearly: `$59.99`

## Launch Notes

- Play Billing products must exist in Google Play Console before purchase buttons activate.
- The current app uses client-side entitlement state for MVP testing only.
- Before shipping, move purchase verification and entitlement state to a secure backend.
