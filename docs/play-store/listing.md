# FieldLedger Play Store Listing

## App Name

FieldLedger

## Short Description

Fast job logging, invoice totals, and payment follow-up for tradespeople.

## Full Description

FieldLedger helps solo tradespeople and field-service operators log work before the details go cold.

Capture the customer, job window, billed materials, costs, and invoice stage in one flow. FieldLedger then turns that into clear weekly revenue, estimated profit, and outstanding payment visibility right on the phone.

This app is built for operators who quote, invoice, and chase payment themselves. It is intentionally simple, offline-first, and focused on the money side of day-to-day field work.

What you can do:

- Log jobs with customer details, work summary, times, and costs
- Preview invoice totals and estimated profit before you save
- Track quotes, sent invoices, and paid jobs in one pipeline
- View weekly revenue, costs, and follow-up counts on the dashboard
- Choose the currency that matches your customers and market
- Keep data local on-device while the product is still in MVP mode

Who it is for:

- Electricians
- Plumbers
- HVAC techs
- Landscapers
- Cleaners
- Handymen and small service crews

Why it stands out:

- Designed for speed on Android phones used in the field
- Focused on billing and follow-up, not bloated office software
- Useful from day one without a team account or desktop setup
